# Mockup Specifications

## 1. AI Mock Interview & Coaching (Lists)
- **Title:** Interview Question Player
- **Description:** A list of audio/video player rows representing interview practice questions.
- **Dimensions:**
    - **Container:** `100%` of grid cell
    - **Row Height:** Fluid (approx 40-50px)
- **Tip:** Use to represent content libraries or practice sessions. The “Play” icon is a universal signifier for interactive content.

## 2. Progress Tracking & Analytics (Charts)
- **Title:** Career Growth Analytics
- **Description:** A visual dashboard featuring a line chart tracking "Interview Readiness" over time and a progress bar for "Weekly Goal" completion.
- **Dimensions:**
    - **Container:** `100%` of grid cell
    - **Chart Height:** Fixed (approx 200px)
- **Tip:** Use gradient fills for charts to make them feel modern. Green and Blue accents are best for conveying positive growth and trust.

## 3. AI Resume & Cover Letter (Document View)
- **Title:** Smart Document Editor
- **Description:** A split-view interface showing a document preview (Resume/CV) with highlighted sections, paired with a floating "AI Suggestion" card or sidebar.
- **Dimensions:**
    - **Container:** `100%` of grid cell
    - **Aspect Ratio:** roughly `1:1.4` (A4 paper shape) for the document preview
- **Tip:** Use skeleton text blocks to represent the document content. Use a "Sparkles" or "Wand" icon to visually indicate AI-powered editing features.
